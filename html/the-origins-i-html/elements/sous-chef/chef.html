Bún Riêu (Vietnamese Crab and Pork Noodle Soup)

🍖 Ingredients

  • 🐔 12 cups Chicken Stock
  • 🦀 4 tbsp Bún Riêu Base
  • 🌴 1 tbsp sugar or 1 palm sugar
  • 🦐 2 tsp fermented shrimp paste (to taste)

🧆 Meatballs

  • 🐷 1 lb ground pork
  • 🦀 6 oz canned crab (drained)
  • 🦀 7 oz crab paste in soybean oil
  • 🥚 two eggs
  • 🧅 three green onions (white parts thinly sliced, green parts chopped)

🥘 Additional Ingredients

  • 🫙 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • 🍅 3-4 tomatoes (quartered)
  • 🫘 one pack of fried tofu
  • 🍜 1 pack vermicelli noodles
  • 🥬 1/2 cabbage (thinly sliced)
  • 🍋 1 lemon/lime

Directions

  1. In a bowl mix crab meat, crab paste, ground pork, eggs, and green parts of the onion.
  2. In a pan, heat up oil and add white parts of the onions, tomatoes and 1 tbsp of soup base. Stir-fry for 2–3 minutes.
  3. Add the chicken stock to a pot. Bring to a boil and add tomato stir-fry mixture. Start dropping spoonfuls (about 1-2 tbsp) of meatball mixture into broth.
  4. Cook for 10 minutes or until the meatballs start to float. Bring down to med/low heat. Season the broth (to taste) with three tbsp Bún Riêu soup base, sugar, and shrimp paste. Add fried tofu, cook for 5 minutes.
